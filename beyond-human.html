Beyond Human

Volume 3 of the Identity Evolution Anthology

"Beyond Human" takes readers on a mind-bending journey into the future of human identity and consciousness. Drawing from quantum physics, transpersonal psychology, and futurism, this final installment of the Identity Evolution Anthology presents a radical new vision of identity that transcends individual boundaries and even the limits of our species.

Book Overview

This groundbreaking work explores how emerging technologies, scientific discoveries, and evolving philosophies are reshaping our understanding of what it means to be human. It challenges readers to consider themselves as part of a larger, interconnected whole - from the quantum level to the cosmic scale. "Beyond Human" is not just a book about the future; it's an invitation to actively participate in shaping that future.
